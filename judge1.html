About the Judges:

Ms. Deepakshi Bhardwaj

From interning with the Interstate and regional task force for housing security of homeless people and Afghan refugees in the state to being a part of the US National Honours' Society, Miss Deepakshi Bharadwaj has always been an inspiration for everyone around her.

A double-major in International Relations and Business Economics from Beloit College, She has been the recipient of various honours globally including the Global Scholars Award, The presidential Award, and The Scholar medal for exemplified seven years of academic achievement and success from DPS Faridabad from where she passed out in 2018.
